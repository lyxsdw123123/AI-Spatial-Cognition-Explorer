The AI agent systematically explores Berlin’s government district by repeatedly scanning for visible points of interest (POIs), selecting the nearest unvisited one, and navigating to it via road-network paths. Across multiple phases, it visited a total of 14 POIs—including cafés, restaurants, banks, and landmarks—progressively exhausting nearby options as distances increased and visible-POI counts decreased (e.g., from 11 down to 0). Despite directional exploration and minor relocations (~150 m), no new POIs were detected after exhausting the initial set; the agent ultimately halted due to reaching its maximum iteration limit—not because exploration was complete or no further POIs exist, but because the current scanning and navigation loop terminated as designed.
